Commission Sizes & Details

Portrait - $200

Bust character with simple background, includes: Speedpaint, changes in sketch version, and small color adjustments in the final version.

Half Body - $300

Illustration of a character in half body format with dynamic pose can includes additional elements such as weapons, complex background or particles.

Full Body / Splash Art / Character Sheet - $400-$600

Includes a full immersed character illustration, can be a Splash Art or a Character design sheet including additional elements.

Terms of Service

- Only 3 slots per month

- Either half or full prepayment. You can pay via invoice or PayPal

- Prices are for personal usage (includes posting online or using as profile picture) commercial usage is additional 50% on base price) This includes using illustrations for selling a product or using them in a project

- Please provide a detailed description of the character, ideally with reference images for character, pose, overall mood, etc Don't be shy!

- Changes are allowed in the sketch stage. Once you confirm the sketch and send full payment, it goes to the rendering stage. You can ask for small tweaks in the end, mostly for colors on the background. Complex changes have additional cost

- Let me know if the commission can be posted online
